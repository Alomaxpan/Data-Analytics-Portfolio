# Customer Behavior Analytics for E-Commerce

## Project Overview

This project analyzes customer behavior data from an e-commerce platform to understand purchasing patterns, customer engagement, and the factors influencing purchase amounts.

The goal of the analysis is to generate business insights that can help improve marketing strategies, pricing decisions, and customer experience.

---

## Business Problem

E-commerce companies rely heavily on data to understand how customers interact with their platforms. 

Key questions addressed in this project include:

- Do premium members spend more than regular customers?
- Which product categories generate the highest revenue?
- Do discounts significantly influence customer purchases?
- What devices do customers use most for shopping?
- How does customer satisfaction relate to spending behavior?

---

## Dataset Description

The dataset contains customer demographics, browsing behavior, and purchase activity.

Main features include:

- **Customer_ID** – Unique identifier for customers  
- **Age** – Customer age  
- **Gender** – Customer gender  
- **Country / City** – Customer location  
- **Device_Type** – Device used for browsing  
- **Browser** – Browser used by the customer  
- **Session_Duration** – Time spent on the website  
- **Pages_Viewed** – Number of pages viewed  
- **Product_Category** – Category of purchased product  
- **Purchase_Amount** – Amount spent by the customer  
- **Discount_Applied** – Whether a discount was applied  
- **Customer_Satisfaction** – Customer satisfaction rating  
- **Membership_Level** – Customer membership tier

## Key Business Insights

1. **Platinum membership customers generate the highest average purchase amount.**

2. **Discounts significantly influence customer purchasing behavior**, suggesting promotional campaigns can increase revenue.

3. Certain **product categories contribute the majority of total sales**, highlighting potential focus areas for marketing.

4. **Mobile devices account for a large portion of customer browsing activity**, indicating the importance of mobile optimization.

5. **Higher customer satisfaction correlates with higher purchase values**, emphasizing the importance of customer experience.
